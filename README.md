# 🛒 E-Commerce Customer Behavior Analytics

An end-to-end Data Analytics project that explores customer purchasing behavior, shopping preferences, revenue patterns, and customer loyalty using Python.

The analysis is based on 20,000 customer records and provides actionable business insights to improve sales, customer retention, and overall platform performance.

---

## 📊 Project Overview

This project focuses on understanding:

- Customer demographics and behavior
- Revenue generation patterns
- Product category performance
- Customer loyalty and repeat purchases
- Cart abandonment behavior
- Payment method preferences
- Device usage trends
- Marketing channel effectiveness

The goal is to transform raw customer data into meaningful business insights and recommendations.

---

## 🎯 Business Objectives

- Identify buyers and non-buyers
- Analyze customer spending behavior
- Discover top-performing product categories
- Understand customer shopping preferences
- Evaluate customer loyalty and retention
- Measure cart abandonment impact
- Explore marketing channel performance
- Provide actionable business recommendations

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

### Customer Segmentation

- 81.3% customers made at least one purchase.
- 18.4% customers registered but never purchased.
- Only 0.3% customers were completely inactive.

### Revenue Insights

- Total Revenue: **$4.49 Million**
- Average Order Value: **$109.14**
- Total Orders: **33,580**

### Customer Demographics

- The **55+ age group** represents the largest customer segment.
- The same group also generates the highest revenue.

### Product Performance

- Beauty category generated **$1.26M**, contributing approximately 28% of total revenue.
- Books and Fashion were the second and third highest-performing categories.

### Customer Loyalty

- 50.2% customers are repeat buyers.
- Most customers belong to Low and Medium CLV segments.

### Shopping Behavior

- Mobile and Desktop orders are almost equally distributed.
- Credit/Debit Card is the most preferred payment method.

### Cart Abandonment

- 3,677 customers abandoned their carts.
- Estimated lost revenue exceeds **$400,000**.

### Customer Engagement

- Only 26.3% of buyers submitted product ratings.
- 55% of customers opted into marketing emails.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### Increase Customer Conversion

- Target non-buyers with welcome offers and discount campaigns.

### Recover Abandoned Carts

- Implement automated email reminders and retargeting campaigns.

### Improve Customer Reviews

- Encourage customers to leave ratings through loyalty rewards.

### Diversify Revenue Sources

- Promote categories beyond Beauty to reduce dependency.

### Optimize Mobile Experience

- Improve mobile checkout and user experience.

### Strengthen Marketing Channels

- Invest more in Email Marketing and Social Media campaigns.
